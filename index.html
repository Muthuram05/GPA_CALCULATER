What is GPA?

There are various types of grading systems followed depending upon your country and college, such as the numbering system, percentage system, or letter grade system. GPA is a type of grading system followed in the US, which tells you how well you have scored in your course on an average as compared to other students. Your GPA is calculated on a scale between 0 to 4.0 and showcases if your grades have been high or low as compared to your class. Moreover, GPA is calculated over a period, i.e., first semester/term.
